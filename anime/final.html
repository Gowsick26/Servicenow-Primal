One Piece

Description

One Piece is a Shonen action-adventure manga and anime series written and drawn by Eiichiro Oda. It is set in a fantasy world dominated by pirates and mainly depicts the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a captain of a crew of pirates. The story revolves around their journey to obtain the ultimate treasure One Piece that was left behind by the King of the Pirates, Gold Roger. Devil Fruit eaters are a devastating weakness on the high seas, and one poor battle on the ocean can send them to their death, or even just a patch of rough water. It is currently unknown why Devil Fruit eaters can't swim. One Piece is a journey that is a gigantic heap of adventures and characters that no one can explain to you in one sentence.

Main characters

  • Monkey D Luffy
  • Roronoa Zoro
  • Nami
  • Ussop
  • Saanji
